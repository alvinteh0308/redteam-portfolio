# Room Name - Red Team Fundamentals

## Objective

The objective is to understand the classification, responsibilities, real-world roles, and workflow of the red team.

## Key Takeaways

- A complete process requires collaboration between red, blue, and white teams.

- Understand the importance of standard cyber kill chains, especially the classic Lockheed Martin Cyber ​​Kill Chain.

- Understand some basic kill chain components, such as reconnaissance, weaponization, delivery, and exploitation.
